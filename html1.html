My college life

Posted on:15-10-2023 Date

The New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) is an autonomous private engineering college located near Marathahalli, in Bangalore, India. Established in 2001, the college is part of the New Horizon Educational Institution which was established in 1970. New Horizon College of Engineering affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) & University Grants Commission (UGC). It is accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade & National Board of Accreditation (NBA). I am proud to tell that i am a student of this college.
